Realm Rules

To keep The Cove a paradise for everyone, we have a few simple guidelines:

  • No griefing, stealing, or destroying others' builds
  • Be respectful and kind to all community members
  • No hacking, cheating, or using exploits
  • Keep chat family-friendly and appropriate
  • Ask before building near someone else's creation
  • Use common areas responsibly and clean up after yourself
  • Report any issues to moderators immediately
  • Most importantly: Have fun and be creative!
